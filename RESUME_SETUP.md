# Resume Setup Instructions

## Adding Your Resume

To enable the resume download feature:

1. **Create your resume** in your preferred format (Word, Google Docs, etc.)

2. **Export as PDF** with filename: `resume.pdf`

3. **Place in root directory**: Copy `resume.pdf` to the portfolio folder (same location as `index.html`)

4. The download button in the hero section will automatically work once the file is in place
